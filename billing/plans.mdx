Every workspace has its own plan. Plans differ in monthly [credits](/billing/credits) and capacity limits — all core features are available on every plan, including auto-learning, analytics, and the calendar.

| | **Free** | **Pro** | **Team** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Credits per month | 100 | 500 | 1,000 |
| Knowledge sources | 5 | 20 | Unlimited |
| Team members | 3 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| White-label bot | — | — | ✓ |

## How to get Pro or Team

- **Pro trial** — every Discord server is offered a one-time 14-day Pro trial. See [Promo codes & trial](/billing/promo-codes).
- **Promo codes** — a code grants a workspace a plan for a fixed number of days.

Your current plan, its remaining days, and your credit balance are always visible on the **Billing** page.

## What happens when a paid plan ends

The workspace falls back to Free. Nothing is deleted — but if you're over the Free limits (e.g. more than 5 knowledge sources), you won't be able to add new ones until you're back under the limit or on a higher plan.
