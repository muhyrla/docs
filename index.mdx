Relayn is an AI community manager for Discord. It sits in your server, watches the channels you choose, and answers members' questions automatically — using the knowledge you give it and the answers it learns from your own moderators.

Communities grow faster than support teams. Relayn closes that gap without hiring.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your server">
    Sign up at [app.relayn.io](https://app.relayn.io) and add Relayn to your Discord server. Takes a few minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add knowledge">
    Upload files, index a web page, or crawl your whole documentation site. This becomes the bot's source of truth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let it answer">
    The bot detects questions in the channels you selected and replies with answers grounded in your knowledge — with links to the sources it used.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch it learn">
    When the bot can't answer, it waits for one of your trusted members to reply — then remembers that answer for next time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
