The **Replies** page in the dashboard is the full history of the bot's work: every question it answered, in reverse chronological order.

For each reply you can see:

- the member's question and the bot's answer,
- when it happened and in which channel,
- which knowledge sources the answer was built from,
- a link to jump to the original message in Discord.

## What to use it for

- **Spot-check quality.** Skim recent replies after adding new knowledge to confirm the bot uses it the way you intended.
- **Trace a bad answer.** If a member reports a wrong reply, find it in the feed, open the sources it used, and fix or delete the offending knowledge.
- **See the bot's value.** Every row in this feed is a question your team didn't have to answer by hand.
