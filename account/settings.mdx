Open **Settings** in the dashboard to manage your account.

## Sign-in

You can sign in with **email + password** or with **Discord**.

- Email accounts must be verified — check your inbox for the verification link after signing up.
- Forgot your password? Use **Forgot password** on the login page to get a reset link.

## Appearance

The dashboard follows your system theme by default; you can pin it to **light** or **dark** in Settings.

## Deleting your account

Account deletion is permanent and removes your access to all workspaces.

- Workspaces where you are the **sole owner and only member** are deactivated: the bot stops working on those servers.
- Workspaces with other members stay with the team.

<Warning>
Deletion can't be undone. If you only want to hand a server over to someone else, invite them to the [team](/workspace/team) first instead of deleting your account.
</Warning>
