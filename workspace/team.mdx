A workspace can be managed by more than one person. Invite teammates on the **Team** page in the dashboard.

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Everything, including changing roles and deleting the workspace. The person who connected the server. One per workspace. |
| **Admin** | Manage workspace settings, invite and remove members, plus everything an editor can do. |
| **Editor** | Manage knowledge and bot settings. Can't touch team or workspace settings. |

Everyone on the team can view the dashboard — replies, analytics, the knowledge base.

## Member limits

| Plan | Team members |
|---|---|
| Free | 3 |
| Pro | Unlimited |
| Team | Unlimited |

## Notes

- Removing a member revokes their dashboard access immediately. It doesn't affect their Discord roles — trusted-member status in [auto-learning](/knowledge/auto-learning) is configured separately in Bot settings.
- Team membership is per-workspace: being an admin on one server grants nothing on another.
