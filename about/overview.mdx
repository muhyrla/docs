Relayn is a SaaS product that plugs an AI-powered support bot into Discord servers. The bot reads your documentation, learns from your moderators' real answers, and handles repetitive community questions 24/7 — so your team can focus on what matters.

Communities grow faster than support teams. Relayn closes that gap without hiring.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Add Relayn to your Discord server via OAuth. Takes under 5 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enrich">
    Upload your docs (`.md`, `.txt`) or index a GitBook site. Relayn chunks, embeds, and stores everything in a vector database.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    The bot starts answering questions immediately. It learns from every moderator response to get smarter over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Architecture at a glance

Relayn is a full-stack application made up of:

- **Backend** — A Django REST API and a Discord bot worker. Handles authentication, workspace and knowledge management, billing, analytics, and the RAG pipeline (document ingestion, embeddings, vector search, and LLM responses via OpenRouter and ChromaDB).
- **Dashboard** — A React (Vite) single-page app where teams manage their workspace: knowledge sources, bot settings, interactions, and team members.
- **Landing and blog** — A Next.js marketing site backed by Sanity CMS.

Under the hood, Relayn relies on PostgreSQL for application data, Redis and Celery for background jobs (like indexing documents and aggregating mod-learned answers), and ChromaDB for vector storage.
